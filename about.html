Welcome to our website! My name is Abhishek and I'm the founder of this simple weather
data service. Our goal is to provide accurate and up-to-date weather information for cities
around the world, with a focus on simplicity and ease of use.

We understand that weather can have a big impact on your daily life, whether you're planning a
trip, going for a run, or just trying to stay comfortable at home. That's why we've designed our
website to make it easy for you to get the weather data you need, without any unnecessary
clutter or confusion.

To use our service, simply enter the name of the city you're interested in and hit "search". Our
website will then retrieve the latest weather data for that city, including temperature, humidity,
and wind speed. We strive to ensure that our data is as accurate and reliable as possible, so you
can trust the information you receive from us.

We're constantly working to improve and expand our service, so if you have any feedback or
suggestions, we'd love to hear from you. You can reach us through our contact page, and we'll
do our best to respond to your message as soon as possible.

Thank you for using our weather data service, and we hope you find the information we provide
useful and informative.
